About Us

In 1893, Bernard Howitzer and his brother Anthony Howitzer founded Bernalceutical with a vision of delivering high grade medical supplies all accross the world. Originally starting off in an abandoned aircraft hangar, the brothers soon made a name for themselves and became pioneers in 19th century medical research. Now more than a hundred years later, Bernalceutical is one of the worlds leading multi-nationals in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology and chemical industries.

Bernalceutical's main headquarters and production facility is situated in the the Shannon Free Trade Zone in a 3000m2 workshop dedicated to labs, testing facilities and a fully integrated assembly line. This allows for the highest standard products to be designed, tested and produced all within our premise. We hire over 25 students annually from all over Ireland and provide more than 300 jobs. Our employees are all highly qualified and have been trained in a first aid course to ensure the safety of all staff. In accordance, we won the company of the year award in 2006, 2009 and 2010.
